# Lexweave Trademark Policy

The Lexweave code is licensed under Apache-2.0 — you can use, modify, and
redistribute it freely, including commercially. This policy covers something
the code license explicitly does not grant (Apache-2.0 §6): the **Lexweave
name and logo**.

The goal is simple: when someone sees "Lexweave", they should know whether
they are getting the genuine project or a third-party derivative.

## You may, without asking

- State facts: "built with Lexweave", "powered by Lexweave", "compatible with
  Lexweave bundles".
- Use the name in articles, talks, tutorials, package descriptions, and
  academic work.
- Distribute unmodified builds of the packages under their original names.
- Maintain forks that clearly present themselves as forks ("a fork of
  Lexweave"), provided the fork's *product name* is different.

## You may not, without written permission

- Name a product, service, app, domain, or company "Lexweave" or something
  confusingly similar (e.g. "LexweavePro", "Lexweave Cloud").
- Use the name or logo in a way that implies official status, endorsement, or
  affiliation.
- Register trademarks, domains, or social handles containing "Lexweave".

## Questions

Open an issue or contact the maintainers for permission requests. We expect to
say yes to community projects that make the ecosystem better.
